Nso nsio ya mbuma na makolo!  Wembi, wembi, eza nso ya kulonga.

(Nkosi, the little baboon, a rambunctious, curious young baboon. Today was an unusual day.  A day of storytelling.)

**Nkosi:** (Nkosikilu), o mama, o papa!  Ebimi luku, ebimi luku!  (Mom, Dad! I have found it! I have found it!)

**Mama Nkosi:** (Yaay!) Wapi, nkwanzi na ngi? (Where, little one? What is it?)

**Nkosi:** Tala, mama.  Nsimba ya bambu, ya mpasi, ya ndolo!  (Look, Mama. A story of hardship, sorrow, and pain.)

**Mama Nkosi:** (Smiling gently) Ndidi wa mbote, Nkwanzi na Nkosi!  (Beautiful one, little Nkosi!)  Lukila bonso mu mbolo! (Tell it like a story!)

**Nkosi:**  Muna mpuku wansala mbeni, kuna nsunga mbi ya mfumu a nzo, ya mbeto... a mfumu!  (There once lived a wicked witch, who lived in a dark hut and on an evil bed… a cruel one!)

**Papa Nkosi:** (Chuckling) Mbeni a nsunga, nkosi?  (A dark witch, Nkosi?)  Sioki!  Lukila nsambu!  (Oh no! Tell us the story!)

**Nkosi:** Ngwiti wina na mbunu wa mvutu... wa diaka banso, na ngolo ya mukongo!  (She was full of tricks... she tricked everyone, and she possessed a frightening strength!)

**Mama Nkosi:**  Ayandi katikila makasi a mfumu wa ndonga?  (Did she steal things from the home?)

**Nkosi:**  Yeye, akete.  Adimbila bantu bonso, na ziku, mabe a ngolo.  (Yes, a bit.  She tricked everyone, cruelly taking their best things.)

**Papa Nkosi:**  Bwandi ya ma mbangi? (Was she punished? Did they catch her?)

**Nkosi:**  (Slightly breathless, clutching his stomach)  O, na zuni, bo bwasala mutambu!   Mbongo, na ndola, ya mfumu!  (Oh, in the heavens, they did create a chase! A struggle and struggle, and the queen was in trouble.)

**Mama Nkosi:**  Wapi bo sambu ya mfumu? (What happened to the queen?)

**Nkosi:** Mbaki na mfumu, nsudi yina ya mpasi, ya ntoto ... Ya mbangi!  (The queen, caught in that terrible trap, a curse came… she cried.)


**Papa Nkosi:**  Nkosi, nkisi yina ya ndola!  (Nkosi, this evil story!)  Lukila, nkwanzi, mbongi mbongi. (Go on, little one,  quickly.)

**Nkosi:**  Na zuni!  Na ntima, ya zulu!  Ntima ya mbeni yina wansala.  (On high! In the sky, in the heart of the witch.)  Sambusai kuluka mbeni!  (She found her tormentor!)


**Mama Nkosi:**  Wapi na ngolo na ndola ya mbogi ya zulu? (Where did she show the power of the sky witch?)

**Nkosi:** Ntembe wina, mpaka mpembe ya mfu!  (With a powerful thunderclap, a flash of white lightning!)  Mbeni akenaka, akenaka na nsa! (The witch trembled, trembled and cried!)


**Papa Nkosi:**  Ebimi yingi!  (It's wonderful!)  Katikila mawa, mbunu ya mbeni ya nsunga!  (And all of this terrible mischief the dark witch caused was nothing.)


**Nkosi:**   Ya mawa, mama, na mpasi na ngolo!  Soki yau nsango mbi, mama.  (Sadness, Mama, it's a dangerous story, Mama. The witch has been beaten.)

**Mama Nkosi:** Ya mbote, nkwanzi.  Lukila bonso mu mbwangu! (Fine, little one, Tell another story swiftly!)

**Nkosi:** (Gazing at his mama, who's eyes shimmer with warmth) Ngwiti, ngwiti wa nkolakasi ... Mbeni wa ngono, mama! (There are also other wicked and cruel spirits.)



**Papa Nkosi:**  Banza diaka! Wapi ngwiti wina, mu nkaka, na mfumu ya nsangu?  (Think of this one! Who was in charge? Was anyone powerful?)



**Nkosi:**  Muntu wa bonso yingina yimani ya ntebi... Ya makasi.  (Someone like a king who was always powerful... Always protected.)


**Mama Nkosi:** Muntu wa nkuma ya dibi.  (Someone who brought calm.)

**Nkosi:** Mbidi ya ngono, kitikila mpasi na ndonga!  (Those who brought protection, stopping bad things from happening.)



**Papa Nkosi:** Ya mbote, nkwanzi.  Ebi mawa mambote! (Good work, little one.  Good lesson.)

**Nkosi:** Eyo, mama na papa! Mbi ma nzo. (Yes, Mother and Father!  The good shall prevail.)

Nso nso ya mbuma na makolo, kulongele!
